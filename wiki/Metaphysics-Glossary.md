## 3. Qi Men Dun Jia (奇门遁甲)

### Structure Overview

A Qi Men chart (盘) arranges 9 palaces (九宫) each containing:
- **Heaven Plate** (天盘) — Celestial Stem layer
- **Earth Plate** (地盘) — Terrestrial Stem layer
- **8 Doors** (八门) — Situational energy
- **9 Stars** (九星) — Cosmic influence
- **8 Gods** (八神) — Spiritual forces
